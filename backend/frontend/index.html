What is Blue Pulse?

Blue Pulse is a comprehensive water-supply monitoring platform designed specifically for rural communities. We bridge the gap between residents and water management authorities through transparent, real-time tracking of water supply issues.

Powerful Features

Everything you need to manage water supply issues efficiently

Submit Complaints

Registered users can easily submit detailed complaints with photos and precise location data. Anonymous visitors can view but not submit reports.

Track Repairs

Monitor real-time status updates, timestamps, and repair progress with photos. Admins keep everyone informed throughout the resolution process.

Admin Tools

Comprehensive admin panel for filtering complaints, updating statuses, managing priorities, and generating detailed analytical reports.
